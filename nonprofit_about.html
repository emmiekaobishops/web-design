NONPROFIT INFO:

We will have already established connections to larger data science organizations for the final steps of the strategic plan as well as easily accessible meeting spaces. One issue that we will have to think about more is attendance and how we might advertise to the greater San Diego community. This includes both middle and high schools with interested students. We can send emails to teachers in these schools and use the few already-established connections we have in order to promote the nonprofit to these institutions. The greatest opportunity for the organization lies in the fact that many middle and high schoolers do not know about the intersection of STEM and humanities fields, and those who wish to pursue both can do so through data science. However, this goes both ways, and many students may only wish to pursue STEM or humanities. Because of this, we must be careful when marketing so as to appeal to all students with a wide variety of interests.
