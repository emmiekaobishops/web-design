WHO ARE WE?

At Data Science for Change, our mission is to educate middle and high school-aged students about the field of data science. More generally, we hope to raise awareness about this field throughout middle and high schools in San Diego to better educate future generations on educated policy-making. This ensures a future of more effective, data-driven laws and policies.
